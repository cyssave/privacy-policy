Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Twitch extension, which integrates with Patreon to display your membership information.

Information We Collect

When you use our Twitch extension, we collect the following information from Patreon:

  • Your Patreon profile image
  • Your Patreon display name
  • Your Patreon email address
  • Your current Patreon membership status

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to enhance your experience within the Twitch extension by displaying your Patreon membership information. This data is used solely for this purpose and will not be shared with third parties or used for marketing.

Data Storage

We do not store your data permanently. All data is fetched temporarily when you use the Twitch extension and is discarded when you stop using the service.

Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at cyssave@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
